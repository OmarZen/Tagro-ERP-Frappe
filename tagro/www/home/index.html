About Tagro Egypt

Founded in 1997, Tagro Egypt is a leading provider of high-quality piping solutions, specializing in PPR, PPH, and UPVC pipes and fittings. With over 25 years of experience, we are committed to delivering superior products that meet the highest international standards.

Word of the CEO

“Tagro means durability and strength in the Egyptian ancient language. For our company, it is more than just a name; it is a way of thinking as we always perceive durability as the quality of our products as the first priority."

- CEO of Tagro Egypt

Our Vision & Mission
